YEMOJA sUSV

Autonomous Surface Patrol Vessel

Autonomous surface navigation

Maritime Robotics autonomous navigation system with COLREGS-compliant operation. Supports full autonomous, remote, and formation modes out of the box. Controllable via VCS Rugged Laptop or Android app, with external 3rd party software integration available.

Persistent maritime surveillance

24-hour endurance at 2 knots on a single battery charge. Rated to Beaufort sea state 4. Designed for unattended extended patrol without operator intervention.

Multi-sensor payload bay

30 kg payload capacity with modular sensor architecture. Standard configuration includes EO camera and AIS Class B receiver. Upgradeable to SeaSight enhanced awareness with AIS Class B transceiver.

RF and acoustic comms

5GHz MIMO radio/WiFi standard. Optional LTE (4G), VHF, and MBR add-ons for multi-layer comms redundancy across contested or degraded RF environments.

Configuration A — Standard

Complete platform with 2× electric motors, 4× lithium-ion batteries, Maritime Robotics autonomous navigation, VCS Rugged Laptop and Android app, EO camera, AIS Class B receiver, and 5GHz MIMO/WiFi. Ready for autonomous or remote operation out of the box.

Configuration B — Enhanced

Standard platform plus SeaSight enhanced situational awareness, AIS Class B transceiver, and SeaCapture data logging. Suited for persistent ISR and data-collection missions requiring full maritime picture.

Operational deployment

Yemoja deploys from vessel, dock, or shoreline and achieves autonomous operation immediately on water contact. Operator monitors and retasks via laptop or Android app. Formation mode supports coordinated multi-USV patrol without additional infrastructure. Optional launch and recovery crane kit and ruggedised flight cases available for expeditionary deployment.

Additional comms

MBR / VHF / LTE (4G) add-ons for multi-layer comms redundancy across contested or degraded RF environments.

Enhanced awareness

SeaCapture data logging

Full-mission data capture and logging software for persistent ISR and post-mission analysis.

Formation mode

Coordinated multi-USV patrol operations without additional ground infrastructure.

Expeditionary kit

Launch and recovery crane kit and ruggedised flight cases for rapid forward deployment from vessel or shore.

Additional integrations

External 3rd party software integration, Autocast SVP winch, and custom payload configurations available on request.
